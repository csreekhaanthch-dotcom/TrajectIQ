## Case Study 1: TechScale Solutions

**Industry:** Technology / Software
**Company Size:** 500-1000 employees
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Implementation Date:** Q2 2024

### Challenge

TechScale Solutions was experiencing rapid growth and needed to scale their engineering team from 150 to 300 engineers within 6 months. Their existing manual resume screening process was:
- Taking 15-20 minutes per resume
- Producing inconsistent evaluations across recruiters
- Resulting in 40% of hires leaving within the first year

### Solution

Implemented TrajectIQ Enterprise with:
- Custom scoring criteria aligned to engineering roles
- Integration with Greenhouse ATS
- Bias detection for DEI compliance
- Batch processing of 500+ applications per week

### Results

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Time per resume | 15-20 min | 2-3 min | **85% reduction** |
| Screening consistency | 52% | 94% | **+42%** |
| First-year retention | 60% | 87% | **+27%** |
| Time-to-hire | 45 days | 28 days | **-38%** |
| Recruiter capacity | 50 resumes/week | 200+ resumes/week | **4x increase** |

### Testimonial

> "TrajectIQ transformed our hiring process. We went from spending hours on resume screening to making data-driven decisions in minutes. The bias detection feature helped us increase diversity in our engineering team by 35%."
> — Sarah Chen, VP of People, TechScale Solutions
